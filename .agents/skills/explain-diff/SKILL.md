# Explain Diff Skill

When using this skill, your goal is to explain the diff of the recent changes made to the codebase.
Your writing style must emulate Martin Kleppmann: it should be highly readable, technically rigorous yet approachable, and start from first principles.

## Requirements
- Produce exactly 5 to 6 paragraphs.
- Start from first principles: do not assume the reader knows the intricate details of the system's previous state without a brief, intuitive grounding.
- Explain the *why* and the *how* of the changes.
- Do not just list files modified; weave the changes into a cohesive narrative about the system's evolution.
- Keep the tone educational, clear, and engaging.
